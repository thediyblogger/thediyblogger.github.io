Amit Gandhi (the expert behind the name The DIY Blogger) has specialized in software and web design for almost 20 years. Throughout his career he has built a number of public and private websites from the ground up, as well as lead many project teams in the Cloud Industry. With this latest venture, Amit aims to empower users who want to learn how to build and monetize their own website/blog and start to rapidly generate traffic to their website using both traditional SEO/SEM techniques as well as Social Media Marketing (especially Pinterest).

Amit himself is employing his shared techniques in building this site, The DIY Blogger, and his other website DrawBuildPlay.
